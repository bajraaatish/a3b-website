## The brief

Boutique hospitality lives or dies on atmosphere. A list of amenities
does not book a room; the *feeling* of the place does. Quiet luxury has
moved into motion design for exactly this reason — restraint reads as
premium.

The brief is a small content set: one hero film, plus the cutdowns the
brand will actually use across a season.

## The approach

The hero film is paced like an arrival. The edit follows the order a
guest experiences the place — the approach, the light through a window,
the small rituals — and never hurries any of it.

- A single cohesive grade runs across every shot, so the set feels like
  one place rather than a highlight reel.
- There is no voiceover and no on-screen text. Ambient sound and the
  framing carry the whole piece.
- The cutdowns are pulled from the same timeline — never re-edited
  louder for social. The restraint is the brand, at every length.

## What shipped

One 2:46 hero film, three social cutdowns between 15 and 30 seconds, and
a silent vertical loop for the booking page.
