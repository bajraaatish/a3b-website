## The brief

A founder origin film is the most common shape a3b is asked for, and the
easiest to get wrong. The instinct is to cover everything — the childhood
detail, the first office, the pivot, the team. The result is a recap reel.

The brief here is the opposite: tell it **once**. One idea, carried the
whole way through. Everything that does not serve that idea is cut.

## The approach

The film is built around a single interview, recorded in one sitting. The
edit's job is restraint — finding the three minutes inside the forty that
actually say something, then holding them longer than feels safe.

- The grade is barely there. Slightly desaturated, blacks lifted toward
  Ink, highlights pulled back from white. Skin tones are left alone.
- Audio leads. The room tone, the pauses, a single sparse music bed
  chosen before the picture was locked.
- Cuts land on stillness — the breath after a sentence, not the start of
  the next one. The viewer's eye is given somewhere to rest.
